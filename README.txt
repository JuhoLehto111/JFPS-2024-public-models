Simulation models of the "Lehto, J., Displacement Control for EM Driven Open Center Mobile Hydraulics, 2024".


Version of MATLAB is 2021B

run_simulation_models.m runs the simulations and plots the results.

Efficiency map of the motor model and geometry model for the multibody have been removed due to confidentiality reasons. Therefore, the simulation won't run, but the model can be viewed.
